## Grading Details

### Accuracy Score (50%)
- (Correct catches ‚àí False positives) √∑ Total errors
- Averaged across all exercises and tests

### Speed Score (30%)
- Based on completion time vs target time for each exercise
- Under target = 100%
- Up to 1.5x target = scaled down
- Over 1.5x target = 50%

### Commitment Score (20%)
- Based on on-time submissions
- Each day: On time = 100%, 1 day late = 90%, 2 days late = 80%, 3+ days late = 70%
- Averaged across all 10 days

### Final Grade
- **A:** 90%+ overall, all submissions within 48 hours
- **B:** 80-89% overall
- **C:** 70-79% overall (pass, but not recommended for freelance)
- **Fail:** Below 70%

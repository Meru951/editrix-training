# Day 2: Training Your Eye

**Subject:** Day 2: The Visual Trick That Catches More Errors

Good morning,

Yesterday you learned what proofreaders do. Today you learn *how* to see errors.

Here's the secret: errors have shapes.

Your brain processes text incredibly fast. Most of the time, that's helpful. When proofreading, it's a problem — your brain autocorrects errors before you consciously notice them.

Today's lesson teaches you to slow that process down.

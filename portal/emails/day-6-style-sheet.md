# Day 6: The Style Sheet

**Subject:** Day 6: The Tool Every Professional Proofreader Uses

---

Good morning,

Today you learn the style sheet — the single most important tool in your proofreading kit.

A style sheet is a running record of every decision in a manuscript: names, places, spellings, formatting choices. It's how you maintain consistency across 300 pages.
